Cuban Panini


Home

Description

With a combination of savory meat and spicy mustard, this sandwich is a delicious take on the classic Cuban deli sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 4 6-inch whole-wheat hoagie rolls, split; 8 slices whole-wheat bread; or 2 whole-wheat pita bread rounds, halved crosswise and split horizontally
  • 4 teaspoons of yellow mustard
  • 6 ounces leftover roast pork tenderloin
  • 2 ounces Black Forest Ham or sliced deli ham
  • 4 slices of Swiss cheese
  • Julienne pickles to taste

Steps

  1. Spread each sandwich bread with 1 teaspoon yellow mustard. Top with 1 1/2 ounces of leftover roast pork and 1/2 ounce of Black Forest Ham. Top with Swiss cheese and julienne pickles.
  2. Lightly coat an unheated panini griddle, covered indoor electric grill, or large nonstick skillet with nonstick cooking spray. Preheat griddle, grill, or skillet over medium heat or heat according to manufacturer's directions. Add sandwiches, in batches if necessary.
  3. If using griddle or grill, close lid and grill for 2 to 3 minutes or until bread is toasted.
  4. If using skillet, place a heavy plate on top of sandwiches. Cook for 1 to 2 minutes or until bottoms are toasted. Carefully remove plate, which may be hot. Turn sandwiches and top with the plate. Cook for 1 to 2 minutes more or until bread is toasted.

Nutrition facts per serving: 380 calories, 24 g protein, 11 g total fat (4 g saturated fat), 49 g carbohydrate, 6 g fiber, 40 mg cholesterol, 700 mg sodium, 72 weighted glycemic index
